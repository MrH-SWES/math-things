Color Logic

The Alternating Sequence

The system radiates from the Ones (Warm Red). It alternates Cool/Warm symmetrically. Large whole numbers and small decimals follow the same rhythmic temperature shift.

Esoteric Migration

Colors are primary and "classroom-familiar" near the decimal point. As you move toward Billions or Thousandths, the hues become more sophisticated and midnight-deep.

The Prime Directive

"No words, no directions."

The apps must be so intuitive that placing them in front of a student results in immediate, correct interaction. We don't explain place value; we provide a world where the laws of physics *are* place value. If a user needs a "Tutorial" button, the design is broken.

Physical Intuition

If a child sees a stack of blocks and an empty slot, they should drag a block. If they see a bead on a wire, they should slide it. The UI is a silent invitation.

Confidence in Hard Things

Math is abstract and difficult. We don't hide that with "fun" distractions. We provide the physical bridge that makes the abstraction manageable.
