Math, in your hands.
The Pedagogy of Physicality

Zero Instructions. Total Intuition.

Math Things. is a suite of hardware-inspired digital manipulatives designed for the Resource Room and the Homeschool desk.

We believe that mathematics is not a set of abstract rules to be memorized, but a physical reality to be experienced. Our tools enforce a "No Words, No Directions" policy. The boundaries of the interface dictate the boundaries of the math.

If a student has to read a paragraph of text to understand a tool, we aren't testing their math—we're testing their executive function. Math Things bypasses the reading barrier entirely, allowing the student to touch the math directly.
